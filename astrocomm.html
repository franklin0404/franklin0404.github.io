Astronomy Club of University Union in Taiwan

I am currently a lecturer in Stargazing Guide Division. Together with other lecturers, we design teaching materials about constellations and give lectures on how to guide the audience in a star gazing event. While we majorly introduce the western and Chinese constellations to people, we are also trying to recover the stories of constellations from the aboriginals in Taiwan.

Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Academia Sinica Paul's Meeting

I was the organizer of Paul's Meeting, a student-organized weekly gathering at the Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Academia Sinica. I invited 17 researchers to give talks on hot topics in various fields over the course of one year and increased the total number of attendees from 6 to 30 per speech through marketing campaigns.
