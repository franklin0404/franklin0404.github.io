Hiking in the Island Full of Mountains

Taiwan is an island full of high mountains. There are 100 mountains over 3000 meters high in Taiwan and the Taiwanese call them Bai-Yue. Together with my colleagues at ASIAA, we often go hiking in those mountains during our vacations. The photo shows us on the summit of Mount Qilai South Peak.
